About Us

Committed to Building India's Clean Energy Future

Atirath Holdings India Limited is a progressive clean energy enterprise headquartered in the vibrant city of Hyderabad, India, and is committed to redefining the nation's energy future through sustainable bioenergy solutions. Established with the vision of fostering a cleaner and greener tomorrow, the company focuses on the development of world-class Compressed Bio-Gas (CBG) infrastructure across the country.

Driven by innovation and sustainability, Atirath Holdings transforms renewable biomass resources, including Napier grass, agricultural residues, and organic waste, into high-quality Compressed Bio-Gas. By harnessing these resources, the company contributes to reducing greenhouse gas emissions, generating rural employment opportunities, and supporting India's transition toward a low-carbon economy.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To architect the definitive benchmark for green gas energy infrastructure across India, transforming regional agricultural challenges into grid-resilient clean energy assets. We envision an economically empowered rural landscape seamlessly integrated with the nation's clean transport ecosystem, driving energy autonomy through robust circular mechanisms.

Our Mission

To deploy state-of-the-art commercial-scale anaerobic digestion processing facilities optimized for stability, safety, and continuous operational performance. By converting unutilized crop residue and organic biomass into bio-methane, we directly contribute to the reduction of structural carbon emissions, mitigate environmental hazard management bottlenecks, and secure commercial-grade distribution networks.

Our Core Values

Circular Economic Focus

We systematically maximize output loops. Process streams simultaneously yield critical carbon-neutral vehicle fuels and ultra-pure, premium organic soil bio-enrichments to rejuvenate localized farming belts.

Regulatory Alignment

Strategically structured to reinforce and accelerate India's sovereign sustainable mandates, specifically driving execution frameworks highlighted within the national SATAT initiative.

Engineering Excellence

Through disciplined technological selection, automated continuous monitoring systems, and standardized raw stock input preparation, we guarantee predictive industrial plant performance models.

Corporate Profile & Core Mandate

As part of its long-term vision, Atirath Holdings aims to establish 50 Compressed Biogas (CBG) Plants with a combined processing capacity of 1000 TPD across the states of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, with an initial target of 500 TPD in each state.

Project Overview

Understanding the scope and vision of India's largest CBG platform

The Atirath proposed Compressed Biogas (CBG) project is structured for a total processing capacity of 1,000 Tons Per Day (TPD), distributed across 50 decentralized plants with a capacity of 20 TPD each across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.

The decentralized architecture enables efficient feedstock collection, reduced transportation costs, stronger local participation, and scalable expansion across multiple districts. The project aims to establish a robust waste-to-energy ecosystem that supports India's clean energy transition and circular economy goals.

Feedstock Supply Strategy

Ensuring reliable and sustainable biomass availability

A reliable and long-term feedstock supply chain is central to the success of the project. To ensure uninterrupted biomass availability, Atirath is actively developing sourcing partnerships with farmers, Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs), biomass aggregators, agricultural cooperatives, and organic waste generators.

Formal sourcing agreements are currently underway to secure agricultural residue, crop stubble, and organic waste streams required for continuous plant operations.

Industrial Processing Sequence

Advanced engineering processes optimized for maximum thermal yield stability.
1.

Sizing & Homogenization

Raw biomass undergoes advanced mechanical processing and biological pre-treatment to clear complex lignocellulosic bonds, preparing materials for high-yield digestion.

2.

Anaerobic Digestion

Homogenized material passes into continuous multi-stage stirred-tank reactors under highly regulated thermal and biological parameters to yield consistent raw biogas outputs.

3.

Membrane Separation

Biogas is purified through selective multi-stage membrane filters to isolate clean, pure automotive-grade Methane (>93% CH4) matching standard IS 16087:2016.

About Atirath Holdings

Atirath Holdings India Limited is a pioneering green energy company headquartered in Hyderabad, spearheading India's clean fuel infrastructure. We are establishing a massive, state-of-the-art 1,000 TPD decentralized Compressed Biogas (CBG) project featuring 50 decentralized 20-TPD plants strategically located across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.

We are looking for an agile, execution-focused Asst HR Manager to join our corporate headquarters in Hyderabad to design, build, and manage our workforce across our corporate office and multiple upcoming plant sites.

Role Overview

  • Role: Asst HR Manager (HR Generalist / Operations)
  • Experience: 4 to 8 years (Manufacturing, infrastructure, renewable energy, or agro-industries preferred)
  • Location: Corporate Office - Hyderabad, with occasional travel to plant sites across AP and Telangana
  • Employment Type: Full-Time, Permanent

Key Responsibilities

1. Greenfield Talent Acquisition & Onboarding

  • Lead end-to-end recruitment to fulfill corporate office needs and technical/blue-collar staffing requirements for 50 decentralized plant sites
  • Source, interview, and hire diverse roles ranging from plant process engineers, supply chain/agribusiness personnel, to local site supervisors and labor
  • Standardize a structured onboarding program that integrates rural and urban workforces into Atirath's corporate values

2. Statutory Compliance & Labor Relations

  • Ensure strict adherence to central and state labor laws, environmental health & safety (EHS) human resource regulations, and factory act guidelines across all plants in AP and Telangana
  • Maintain all statutory records, contract labor registrations, and employment filings seamlessly
  • Oversee conflict resolution and foster positive relations with local rural communities, farm partners, and plant workforces

3. HR Operations & Strategy Alignment

  • Partner with the Leadership to design and execute HR strategies that scale smoothly from 1 plant to 50 plants
  • Implement and manage HRMS software platforms to track attendance, payroll inputs, and performance management metrics across geographically dispersed teams
  • Formulate clear, fair, and scalable company-wide HR policies, code of conduct, and employee benefit programs

4. Training & Development

  • Collaborate with technical and plant heads to design and implement specialized training modules regarding CBG plant safety, biomass conditioning, and operational excellence

Key Skills & Requirements

  • Education: UG (Any Graduate), PG (MBA / PGDM in Human Resources or related field preferred)
  • Core Skills: Talent Acquisition, Statutory Compliance, Labor Laws (AP & Telangana frameworks), Employee Relations, HR Policy Formulation, HRMS/HR Software management, Contract Labor Management
  • Language Proficiency: Fluency in Telugu and English is highly critical to effectively manage local plant labor, government liaisons, and rural stakeholders
  • Attributes: Strong leadership, cross-functional communication, conflict resolution, and the ability to operate comfortably in a fast-paced, evolving startup environment
