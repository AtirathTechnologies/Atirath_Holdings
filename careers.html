Asst Manager HR

Join Atirath's HR team to build and manage workforce across 50 CBG plant sites in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana

Location
Hyderabad, Telangana
Experience
4-8 Years
Employment Type
Full Time, Permanent

About Atirath Holdings

Atirath Holdings India Limited is a pioneering green energy company headquartered in Hyderabad, spearheading India's clean fuel infrastructure. We are establishing a massive, state-of-the-art 1,000 TPD decentralized Compressed Biogas (CBG) project featuring 50 decentralized 20-TPD plants strategically located across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. As we enter a rapid scaling and construction phase, Atirath is committed to driving rural economic growth and sustainable energy transition at an unprecedented scale.

We are looking for an agile, execution-focused Asst HR Manager to join our corporate headquarters in Hyderabad to design, build, and manage our workforce across our corporate office and multiple upcoming plant sites.

Role Overview

  • Role: Asst HR Manager (HR Generalist / Operations)
  • Experience: 4 to 8 years (Experience in manufacturing, infrastructure, renewable energy, or agro-industries is highly preferred)
  • Location: Corporate Office - Hyderabad, with occasional travel to plant sites across AP and Telangana
  • Employment Type: Full-Time, Permanent

Key Responsibilities

1. Greenfield Talent Acquisition & Onboarding

  • Lead end-to-end recruitment to fulfill corporate office needs and technical/blue-collar staffing requirements for 50 decentralized plant sites
  • Source, interview, and hire diverse roles ranging from plant process engineers, supply chain/agribusiness personnel, to local site supervisors and labor
  • Standardize a structured onboarding program that integrates rural and urban workforces into Atirath's corporate values

2. Statutory Compliance & Labor Relations

  • Ensure strict adherence to central and state labor laws, environmental health & safety (EHS) human resource regulations, and factory act guidelines across all plants in AP and Telangana
  • Maintain all statutory records, contract labor registrations, and employment filings seamlessly
  • Oversee conflict resolution and foster positive relations with local rural communities, farm partners, and plant workforces

3. HR Operations & Strategy Alignment

  • Partner with the leadership team to design and execute HR strategies that scale smoothly from 1 plant to 50 plants
  • Implement and manage HRMS software platforms to track attendance, payroll inputs, and performance management metrics across geographically dispersed teams
  • Formulate clear, fair, and scalable company-wide HR policies, code of conduct, and employee benefit programs

4. Training & Development

  • Collaborate with technical and plant heads to design and implement specialized training modules regarding CBG plant safety, biomass conditioning, and operational excellence

Key Skills & Requirements

  • Education: UG (Any Graduate), PG (MBA / PGDM in Human Resources or related field preferred)
  • Core Skills: Talent Acquisition, Statutory Compliance, Labor Laws (AP & Telangana frameworks), Employee Relations, HR Policy Formulation, HRMS/HR Software management, Contract Labor Management
  • Language Proficiency: Fluency in Telugu and English is highly critical to effectively manage local plant labor, government liaisons, and rural stakeholders
  • Attributes: Strong leadership, cross-functional communication, conflict resolution, and the ability to operate comfortably in a fast-paced, evolving startup environment
