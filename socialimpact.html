Make in India Initiative

  • Indigenous Technology: CBG plants using locally manufactured equipment and Indian engineering
  • Domestic Feedstock: 100% locally sourced agricultural residue and Napier Grass
  • Indian Workforce: Complete employment of Indian nationals across all operations
  • Atmanirbhar Bharat: Reducing dependence on imported fossil fuels

Import Reduction Impact

  • Fossil Fuel Substitution: Each plant displaces 7,000+ liters of diesel/CNG daily

  • Energy Security: Enhancing India's energy independence through renewable domestic production

Common Questions

Investment FAQs

Minimum investment amount?
Direct investment starting from ₹5 lakh. Land investment from ₹3 lakh per acre. Self agriculture requires minimum 0.5 acre of land.
What are expected returns?
12-15% IRR for direct investment. Land lease yields 8-10% annual return + land appreciation (estimated 7-10% p.a.). Buyback provides 2-3x income vs traditional crops.
Is my investment secured?
Land investment gives you absolute ownership. Buyback agreement is legally binding with 15-year contract. Direct investment has government SATAT scheme backing.
Contract duration?
Land lease: 15 years (renewable). Buyback agreement: 5-15 years. Direct investment: Flexible exit after 5 years.
Who manages the farming?
For land lease option: Atirath manages complete cultivation. For self agriculture: You manage with our technical support and training.
Price assurance?
Buyback agreement has fixed minimum price with annual escalation. Land lease has fixed annual lease amount with 5% increase every 3 years.
