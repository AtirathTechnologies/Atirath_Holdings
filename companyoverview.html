Atirath Holdings India Limited

As part of its long-term vision, Atirath Holdings aims to establish 50 Compressed Biogas (CBG) Plants with a combined processing capacity of 1000 TPD across the states of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, with an initial target of 500 TPD in each state.

The company is actively progressing strategic collaborations and Memorandums of Understanding (MoUs) with the Governments of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana to strengthen the clean energy ecosystem.

Aligned with the Viksit Bharat vision of the Government of India, Atirath Holdings is dedicated to promoting rural prosperity, generating sustainable livelihoods, reducing carbon emissions, and contributing to India's long-term net-zero and energy independence goals.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To emerge as India's leading clean energy infrastructure company by developing world-class Compressed Biogas (CBG) ecosystems, driving the nation's transition towards energy independence, carbon neutrality, and a sustainable future in alignment with the Viksit Bharat vision.

Our Mission

To establish 50 large-scale CBG plants across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, transform agricultural and organic waste into clean renewable energy, empower rural communities, create sustainable livelihoods, and contribute significantly towards India's net-zero and energy security goals.

Opportunity to Aspiring Entrepreneurs

Atirath Holdings invites passionate and driven entrepreneurs to join India's clean energy revolution. Partner with us to build a sustainable future while creating lasting impact in rural communities.

Strategic Partnerships

Collaborate with Atirath Holdings as a strategic partner to establish CBG plants across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. Leverage our technical expertise, project management capabilities, and government partnerships to build a profitable clean energy venture.

CBG Plant Franchise

Become a franchise partner and set up your own CBG production facility under the Atirath brand. Benefit from our proven business model, technical support, feedstock supply chain, and SATAT-aligned offtake agreements.

Feedstock Supply Network

Join our supply chain as a feedstock aggregator. Source and supply agricultural residues, Napier Grass, and organic waste to our CBG plants. Build a sustainable business while supporting rural farmers and waste management.

CBG Retail Outlet

Establish CBG retail outlets and become a part of India's growing clean fuel ecosystem. With increasing demand for sustainable fuels, this is the perfect opportunity to build a future-ready business.
