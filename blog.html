Atirath Holdings Commits ₹8000 Crore for 50 CBG Plants combined setup in Telangana & Andhra Pradesh

June 26, 2026 Atirath Insights Government Policies

Atirath Holdings India Limited has signed strategic MoUs with the Governments of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana to establish 50 Compressed Biogas (CBG) plants with a combined processing capacity of 1000 TPD. With an initial target of 500 TPD in each state, this ₹8000 crore investment is set to transform India's clean energy landscape, creating over 9000 jobs and contributing to the Viksit Bharat vision.
